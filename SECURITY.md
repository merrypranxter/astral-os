## Incident Response

### Level 1: Minor Anomaly

**Indicators:** Single signature, brief duration, no escalation

**Response:**

### Level 2: Moderate Threat

**Indicators:** Multiple signatures, persisting hours, mild interference

### Level 3: Critical Threat

**Indicators:** Coordinated attack, reality distortion, physical symptoms
