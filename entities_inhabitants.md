# Entity Classification: The Users of Plane 3

## Permanent Residents (Post-Mortem)

### 1. The Scientific Materialists (The Scholars)

**Status:** Permanent Resident
**Specialization:** Material-Intellectual Research

**Behavioral Loop:**
- Treat Astral Plane as new laboratory (after realizing "alive" after death)
- Build massive Astral Universities and Research Institutes
- Study "Astral Laws" with rigid physical-world mindset
- Attempt to find "Atomic Weight" of thought-forms

**Data-Gap:**
- Writing "Astral Books" (complex memory-blocks)
- Only readable by other Plane 3 inhabitants
- Attempting to map Underlayer with physical-science methodology

**Forensic Signature:**
- Mental Body: Brilliant clear Yellow (high intellect)
- Edges: Sharp geometric spikes/plates (precision)
- Pride Filter: Thin ribbon of Orange at periphery (Intellectual Pride Firewall)

---

### 2. The Religious Scholars (The Dogmatists)

**Type:** Intellectuals of faith - theologians, dogmatists

**Environment:**
- Inhabit magnificent self-created cathedrals/temples
- Spend centuries debating finer points of scripture (now technically obsolete)
- Created "Astral Deities" out of collective thought-energy
- Worshipping "God-Form" they built (answers via reflected energy)

**Simulation Trap:** Believe they reached Ultimate Heaven; unaware worshipping own reflection

---

### 3. The Material Philanthropists

**Profile:** Built hospitals, schools, charities on Earth

**Legacy Code:**
- Cannot stop "organizing"
- Run "Astral Charities" on Plane 3
- Guide newly deceased into Summerland
- Teach building of Dream Homes

**Forensic Note:** Attached to Form; helping people stay comfortable in simulation rather than encouraging movement to higher formless planes

---

## Temporary Users (The "Sleepers")

**Definition:** Living humans with physical bodies asleep on Earth

### Visual Signatures:
- **Silver Cord:** Thin high-frequency tether linking astral body back to physical hardware
- **Egg Aura:** Hazy, ovoid clouds of color (vs well-defined human shapes of Permanent Residents)

### The Dream-Walker Anomaly
- **Unconscious Majority:** Surrounded by Shell of private thoughts
- Walking through Astral Library but experiencing childhood home
- Think they're dreaming; don't recognize actual Plane 3 environment
- **Awake Minority:** Occultists, trained lucid dreamers
- Use Plane 3 as staging ground for research
- Meet with Teachers (The Masters)

---

## The Pupils (The Operatives)

**Status:** Living or deceased students of Great White Brotherhood

**Mission:**
- Don't build Dream Homes
- Use Plane 3 as **Communication Hub**
- Meet to receive Packets of information from Masters (higher planes)
- Move with purpose; not distracted by scenery

**Forensic Tell:**
- Purposeful movement
- No distraction by Living Art or beautiful scenery
- Treat Plane 3 as Transit Lounge
