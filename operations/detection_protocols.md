**Validation:** If you cannot find a logical resolution after 3+ analysis attempts = likely Ahi interference

#### 2. Sudden Vibrational Coldness

**Signature:** Sharp drop in "Vibrational Temperature" of local node (room/environment)

**Measurement:** Subjective feeling of "cold" unrelated to physical temperature

**Characteristics:**

- Others in same space may not perceive it
- Accompanied by "heavy" feeling

**Differentiation from Physical Cold:**
- Physical cold affects body surface
- Vibrational cold affects "energy body" or mood
- Physical cold responds to blankets/heaters
- Vibrational cold persists regardless of physical measures
