# 7th Sub-Plane Topography & Phenomena
## The Landscape of The Dregs

---

## GEOGRAPHY

### The Underground Reality
- **Location**: Within solid crust of Earth following gravity laws
- **Sensation**: Infinite claustrophobia - moving through solid rock with grinding friction
- **Darkness Paradox**: Not pitch black - heavy luminous light like liquid soot
- **Visibility**: Can see only few feet in front of you

### The Slag-Heaps
**The Ground**:
- Semi-solidified tar mixed with broken glass
- Astral reflection of Earth's crust thickened by heavy thoughts

**The Buildings (Habit-Shells)**:
- Not architecture but exuded structures
- Created when groups obsess over locations (prisons, execution sites, slums)
- Grey, windowless, "sweating" with astral condensation
- Permanent flickering shadows of physical places

**The Vegetation**:
- No life - only Fungoid Thought-Forms
- Black oily lichen growing on habit-shell walls
- Pulse in sync with collective despair of inhabitants

## PHENOMENA

### The Desire-Fire
**Mechanism**: Thoughts crystallize into visible geometry
- Thought of hatred doesn't pass - becomes sharp jagged geometry
- Can physically "cut" or "bruise" other astral bodies
- Environment constructed from sharp unyielding thoughts

### The Hook Mechanic
- Thoughts are not clouds but **hooks**
- Hateful thought forms hooked, barbed structure
- Snags astral mantle on contact
- Inhabitants clumped in tangled masses of angry vibrations

### The Clumping Effect
- "Like attracts Like" is physical law
- Inhabitants compressed by collective vibration weight
- Form "Masses of Suffering" looking like single writhing organism from distance

### The Astral Miasma
**The Pressure**:
- Weight of all unfulfilled physical desires pressing down
- Makes movement slow-motion and exhausting

**Desire-Storms**:
- Massive waves of emotion from physical world (riots, wars, global panic)
- Manifest as "Black Cyclone" of grit and red sparks
- Scour entities stripping outer layers leaving them raw and screaming

### The Mirage of the Physical
- Ghosts of physical world: bottles, gold, weapons
- Vibrational echoes looking real
- Dissolve on touch into:
- Bitter ash
- Foul-smelling liquid
- Constant "tease" of unfulfilled craving

### The Second Death (The Rot)
**The Mechanism**:
- Astral body not permanent - decays by losing vital particles
- Entity becomes greyer and thinner
- Eventually looks like skeletal frames of smoke

**The End-State**:
- Ego can no longer hold heavy matter together
- Astral body **shatters**
- Ego withdraws to 6th plane
- Described as moment of relief after centuries of burning

### The Grey Void Pockets
- Vibration so low mimics non-existence
- Data unreadable - no Light (Intelligence) to illuminate Matter (Density)

### The Sound of the Void
- **The Grind**: Constant metal-on-metal from tectonic plates
- **The Whisper-Gallery**: Sound-thoughts travel slowly but clearly

## UNDER-EARTH CITIES

### The Agartha Reflection (Shadow-Polis)
**Note**: Not romanticized Agartha - grim tectonic architecture

**Location**:
- Inside solid granite and basalt
- Ordered vibration resisting chaotic grind

**Visuals**:
- Cyclopean monolithic structures
- Massive windowless pyramids
- Obsidian-black towers grown not built
- No sky - ceiling is pulsing phosphorescent vein (livid violet or cold blue)

**Surface**:
- Vitrified glassy impossibly smooth
- Astral matter compressed to diamond hardness

---

### Gnome-Lords Architecture
**Function**: Switchboards of the Mineral Kingdom

**Resonance Chambers**:
- Vast circular halls amplifying Earth's hum
- Manage crystal growth and tectonic pressure in physical world

**Sensory**:
- **Sound**: Constant rhythmic thumping like heartbeat of titan
- **Effect**: Vibration so intense feels like unmaking your astral form
- **Smell**: Ancient ozone, cold iron, "Deep Time"

---

### Remnant Enclaves (Pre-Human Residents)
**Inhabitants**: Asuras of Earth - remnants of first and second Root Races

**Visuals**:
- Colossal statues of living stone
- Don't move for centuries
- Plugged into city architecture

**Function**: Living batteries

**Aura**: Refractive - bend murky light like prism creating visual glitches

**Communication**: Seismic bursts of thought - heavy crushing blocks of information

---

### The Vril Veins (Power Source)
**Location**: Built along ley-lines of 7th plane

**The Black Sun**:
- Vortex of pure gravity at center
- Hole in astral fabric
- Provides energy keeping city from being crushed

**Taste**: Alkaline battery acid and bitter almonds

---

### Museums of the Lost
**Function**: Physical backup for Akashic record due to 7th plane stability

**Shadow-Casts**:
- Perfect copies of extinct cities
- Lower levels of Nineveh, foundations of lost Lemurian spires
- Preserved in 7th-plane matter

**Statue-Ghosts**:
- Humans who died in deep mines or caves
- Shades magnetically locked wandering geometric halls
- Blind, flickering, eternal

## CASE STUDIES

### The Miser's Loop
- Entities spending centuries in 4x4 foot space
- Magnetically anchored to specific object (gold box, deed)
- Only see grey fog and hyper-bright reflection of their object

### The Vivisectionist's Purgatory
- Those who took pleasure in cruelty
- Thought-forms of victims permanent fixtures
- Don't attack - simply observe with thousands of unblinking eyes
- Effect: Absolute surveillance terror

### The Addict's Reach
- 7th plane entity touching living person in bar
- Hand looks like wisp of dirty smoke
- Becomes hardened claw entering living aura
- Phase-shift from gas to solid

## ANOMALIES

### The Un-Lights
- Pockets of "Dark Light" - vibrations making things harder to see
- Appearance: Black fire

### The Weight-Stones
- Chunks of 6th plane matter falling into 7th
- Float upward but hot to touch
- Emit "screaming" vibration as crushed by 7th density

### Depth Sickness
- **Cause**: Too long in under-earth geometries
- **Effect**: Vibrational calcification - astral body hardens taking on metallic stink
- **Consequence**: Rising to 6th plane impossible without shattering dissolution
