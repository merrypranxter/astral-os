## VISUAL (THE SIGHT)

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex Code | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Dried Blood | #3E1E1E | Primary gradient base |
| Void-Black | #1A0A0A | Absolute darkness |
| Lurid Red | #7F0000 | Clotted blood, dying furnace |
| Brownish-Grey | - | Wet ash, tint of selfishness |
| Livid Violet | - | Phosphorescent ceiling veins |
| Cold Blue | - | Under-earth illumination |

**Predominant Hues**: Lurid brownish-reds, sickly sulfur-yellows, heavy bruised purples

### Atmospheric Conditions
- **Soot-Mist**: Thick particulate suspension flecking field of vision
- **Lighting**: No sun - illumination from "Black Fire" (un-lights) and phosphorescent veins
- **Sky-Ceiling**: Flat oppressive plane of dark grey vibration, few hundred feet up
- **Sensation**: Like being in low-ceilinged basement stretching forever

### Object Properties
- **Opaque-Plus**: Don't just block light, absorb it aggressively
- **Visual Burn-In**: Objects stay in vision as black silhouettes after looking away
- **Texture**: Jagged, blurred, coated in slime of low-vibration thought
- **Transparency Error**: Unlike higher planes, matter absorbs rather than transmits

### Entity Visuals
- **Auras**: Heat shimmer coming off hot asphalt, filled with black oily flecks
- **Animalization**: Faces lose human definition, jaws heavy, eyes predatory

## AUDITORY (THE SOUND)

### Ambient Soundscape
| Sound | Description | Source |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Tectonic Grind | Constant low-frequency metal-on-metal | Earth's plates reflected in astral ether |
| Bubbling | Simmering pot of thick liquid | Internal chemical processes |
| Low Hum | Distant rhythmic thudding | Heartbeat of Earth's internal fires |
| Planetary Hum | Sub-audible transformer sound | The Law being enforced |

### Speech Characteristics
- **Syllable-Lag**: Viscous communication - thoughts take time to travel through dense matter
- **Quality**: Speech in vacuum - distorted, elongated, muffled
- **Whisper-Gallery**: Sound-thoughts travel slowly but clearly - hear screams from miles as cold wind

### Anomalous Sounds
- **Hate-Hooks**: High-pitched metallic hiss like steam escaping pressurized valve
- **Terror-Forms**: Low-frequency thrum inducing sense of impending doom
- **Acoustic Shadow**: Sound inverted when hiding - hear inside own head louder than outside
- **Death-Rattle**: Constant bubbling of gases through rock
