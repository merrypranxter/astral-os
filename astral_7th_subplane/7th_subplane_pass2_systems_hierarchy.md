- **Goal**: Mass control, energy harvesting

**Detection**:

**Egregores** (4th plane):
- **Collective** thought-forms
- **Inject** nightmares into sleepers
- **Maintain** baseline fear (highest yield energy)

**Shells** (7th plane):
- **Individual** harvesters

- **Gravitate** to trauma nodes
