# THOUGHT-FORMS: OBJECTS OF THE MENTAL PLANES

## Definition
Thought-forms are **crystallized mental energy** — ideas given sufficient intensity and duration to achieve semi-autonomous existence within the plane-appropriate medium.

---

## 7TH PLANE: CONCRETE PROTOTYPES

### Permanent Prototypes
**Nature**: When physical-plane architect dreams perfect cathedral, or coder dreams perfect algorithm, creates **permanent prototype** in 7th

**Composition**: Woven glass and white fire
- Translucent but structurally definite
- Pulsing rhythmic "thrum" like distant engine

**Tactile Quality**: Logic, not material
- Touching machine-thought = feel how parts work together
- Sensory experience of "function" not cold steel

**Stability**: Unlike Astral (shifts with every whim), 7th forms are stable
- Vibrate with persistent frequency
- Only change when deliberate logical modification applied

### The Bliss-Ovoids
**Nature**: Human egos in Devachanic sleep between incarnations
**Appearance**: Large luminous eggs, white and rose light
**Function**: Processing "good data" from previous life into permanent faculty
**Sensory**: Profound unshakeable satisfaction — "Job Well Done" × 1000

## 6TH PLANE: RECURSIVE SIMULATIONS

### Living Tableaus
**Appearance**: Frozen moments of domestic bliss
- Mother holding child, lovers walking, etc.

**Reality**: Not ghosts of actual people
- **Recursive Thought-Simulations** — looping memory-packets
- Created by souls who loved deeply, now processing that love

**Tactile**: Burst of pure crystalline tenderness on touch
**Auditory**: Echoes of kind words decades past — silver bells against velvet

### God-Forms
**Appearance**: Massive shimmering statues (Christ, Krishna, Buddha, etc.)
**Nature**: **Collective Thought-Aggregates**
- Thousands of minds projecting same image build stable semi-permanent structure
**Function**: Peer-to-Peer Wisdom Network
- "Answers" using combined wisdom of all creators

### Karmic Threads
**Appearance**: Hair-fine glowing filaments extending from ovoids
**Function**: Literal cables of connection
**Vibration**: Pulse golden when soul thinks of loved one
**Tone**: Harp strings — specific frequencies per relationship type
- Mother-Son: Deep resonant cello
- Lover-Lover: High shimmering violin

**Creation** (4th-7th): Intensity of thought crystallizes plane-appropriate structure

**Return** (2nd-1st): Data released back to source vortices
**Re-manifestation**: Cycle repeats with new initialization parameters
