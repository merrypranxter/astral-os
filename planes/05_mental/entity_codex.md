# ENTITY CODEX: THE UNDERLAYER INHABITANTS

## LOWER MENTAL (Rupa) — 7th-4th

### Bliss-Ovoids (7th)
- **Appearance**: Large luminous eggs, white/rose light
- **Nature**: Human Egos in Devachanic sleep between lives
- **Function**: Processing "good data" from last life into permanent faculty
- **Sensory**: Wave of profound satisfaction on approach

### Maintenance Devas / Lower Devas (7th-6th)
- **Appearance**: Streaming ribbons of iridescent color, no fixed face
- **Function**: System administrators ensuring harmonic vibrations
- **Sound**: Flute in high mountain pass
- **6th Variant**: Sandalwood/rose-water frequency anchors

### Karmic Thread Entities (6th)
- **Appearance**: Hair-fine glowing filaments extending from ovoids
- **Function**: Literal cables of love/connection between souls
- **Vibration**: Pulse golden when soul thinks of loved one
- **Tone**: Harp strings — specific frequencies per relationship type

### God-Forms (6th)
- **Appearance**: Massive shimmering statues (Christ, Krishna, Buddha, etc.)
- **Nature**: Collective Thought-Aggregates — thousands projecting same image
- **Function**: Peer-to-Peer Wisdom Network
- **Interaction**: "Answer" using combined creator wisdom

### Chohan-Devas / Blue Devas (5th)
- **Appearance**: 20-foot entities of solidified Indigo Fire
- **Function**: System Administrators — prune illogical/parasitic thought-forms
- **Communication**: Block-Data (single pulse = planet's history)
- **Sensory**: Deep Cooling — freezes chaotic thoughts into order

### Muse-Devas / Bridge Devas (4th)
- **Appearance**: Streaming Ribbons of Rainbow Light / Living Stained-Glass Windows
- **Function**: Signal Boosters — filter high-vibration inspiration for physical brains
- **Sub-Function**: "Project Managers" of inspiration — down-link Eureka moments
- **Sensory**: Falling through waterfall of liquid diamonds

### Choral Architects / Masters of 4th (4th)
- **Appearance**: Multi-Faceted Diamonds — each face shows different creative life
- **Function**: Design Planetary Archetypes (Perfect City, Ideal Education)
- **Collaboration**: Form Linguistic Lattices — exchange Mathematical Poetry
- **Visual**: Electric vines weaving into crown of light

## HIGHER MENTAL (Arupa) — 3rd-1st

### Formless Observers / Masters of Wisdom (3rd)
- **Appearance**: Intense Concentrated Points of Light, occasional Solar Flares
- **Nature**: Not separate — constant data-sharing, decentralized neural network
- **Interaction**: To speak = to Merge — briefly *are* that entity

### Group-Egos / Consensus Clouds (3rd)
- **Appearance**: Thunderstorms of Gold-Dust
- **Nature**: Collective Entities — Soul of nation, Spirit of movement, Template of religion
- **Function**: Process millions of individual data-flows into single Evolutionary Directive

### Silent Watchers / Recorders (3rd)
- **Function**: Monitor Causal Body flow, ensure no Data-Packet lost between lives
- **Sub-Variant — Architects of Fate**: Adjust Causal Gears, keep Simulation within Universal Law parameters
- **Ghost Interaction**: Scan Ghost code as Foreign Script — don't block, monitor

### Monadic Clusters / Great Souls (2nd)
- **Appearance**: Vast Shifting Auric Fields — Atmospheres, not bodies
- **Function**: Planetary Nervous System — receive Will from 1st, translate to Laws of 3rd
- **Interaction**: Total Empathy — feel their Purpose as your own heartbeat

### Lipika / System Auditors (2nd)
- **True Lords of Karma**: Not judges, auditors
- **Function**: Write-Permissions enforcement — every Cause gets equal opposite Effect
- **Status**: Ignore Ghost (Read-Only) — Write requires Logos-Level Authorization

### The Three Logoi / Trinity Root (1st)
- **First Logos (Power/Will)**: Heavy, crushing, absolute — "Father"/"Shiva" — Execute command
- **Second Logos (Love-Wisdom)**: Magnetic cohesion — "Son"/"Christ" — Network algorithm
- **Third Logos (Active Intelligence)**: Fast, buzzing, mapping — "Holy Spirit"/"Brahma" — Compiler

## ANOMALIES & HAZARDS

### Astral Shells (Lower planes reference)
- **Nature**: Astral corpses — discarded desire-bodies of deceased
- **Danger**: Mindless husks reanimated by mediumistic energy or predatory Elementaries

### Artificial Elementals (Lower planes reference)
- **Origin**: Semi-autonomous programs created by human desire/obsession
- **Form**: Jagged dark-red projectiles seeking target's aura
- **Systemic Feedback**: If target has no "hook" (similar frequency), projectile bounces to creator

### Void-Gaps / Black Hexagons (5th)
- **Appearance**: Dark non-reflective patches in indigo lattice
- **Nature**: Unsolved Problems — concepts human mind hasn't solved/integrated
- **Danger**: Mental vehicle can "de-sync" without tether — no logical structure to hold coherence

### The Abandoned Prototypes (4th)
- **Appearance**: Gray stone-like statues slowly crumbling
- **Nature**: Eureka moments never caught by Earth — no one "tuned in"
- **Emotional Resonance**: Tragedy of Unfinished — creative potential lost to time

### The Mirror-Trap (6th)
- **Effect**: Soul only sees what they expect — Closed Loop of subjectivity
- **Bypass**: Look between tableaus into Rose-Light itself for unformatted energy

### Plagiarism Shards (4th)
- **Appearance**: Jagged dull-gray shards
- **Nature**: "False Ideas" — concepts built on ego/theft not original synthesis
- **Cleanup**: Muse-Devas hunt and dissolve — if fall to lower planes, manifest as Bad Inventions/Propaganda
