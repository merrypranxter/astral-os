# DEVA TAXONOMY: SYSTEM ENTITIES OF THE MENTAL PLANES

## Classification by Function

### MAINTENANCE DEVAS (7th-6th)
**Lower Devas / System Administrators**

**Appearance**: Streaming ribbons of iridescent color, no fixed face
**Environment**: Golden mist (7th), rose-light (6th)
**Function**: Ensure sub-plane vibrations remain harmonious
**Sound**: Flute in high mountain pass
**Interaction**: Background process — rarely directly perceived

**6th Plane Specialization**: Frequency anchors
- Maintain sandalwood/rose-water scent signature
- Calibrate 98.6°F temperature constant
- Prevent shock to recovering souls

### GUARDIAN DEVAS (5th)
**Chohan-Devas / Blue Devas / Lords of Meditation**

**Appearance**: 20-foot entities of solidified indigo fire
**Environment**: Electric indigo void, hexagonal lattice
**Function**:
- Prune illogical/parasitic thought-forms

- Maintain structural integrity of logic-layer

**Communication**: Block-Data transmission
- Single pulse = entire planet's history
- No words or images — pure data packets

**Sensory Effect**: Deep Cooling
- Approaching entity freezes chaotic thoughts into order
- "Mental lungs" cleansed by ozone

**Origin**: Human obsessive desire/obsession
**Form**: Semi-autonomous programs, often jagged dark-red projectiles
**Behavior**: Seek target's aura; bounce to creator if no frequency match
**Systemic Role**: Feedback loop — teaching consequences of toxic thought

### Astral Shells (Astral reference)
**Nature**: Discarded desire-bodies of deceased
**Danger**: Mindless, drifting; can be reanimated by mediumistic energy or predatory entities

### Plagiarism Shards (4th plane anomaly)
**Appearance**: Jagged dull-gray shards
**Origin**: Ideas built on ego/theft rather than original synthesis
**Weight**: Heavy — drag downward through planes
**Cleanup**: Muse-Devas hunt and dissolve
**Escape Risk**: If reach lower planes, manifest as bad inventions/propaganda

### Void-Gaps (5th plane hazard)
**Appearance**: Black hexagons in indigo lattice
**Nature**: Unsolved problems — no logical structure
**Danger**: Consciousness de-sync if entered untethered
