## VIBRATION HIERARCHY (The Unseen Structure)

### Core Principle
Everything in the astral operates on **vibrational frequency**, not material composition. The 7th plane is "low" not in position but in **oscillation speed**.

### The Scale
```
Higher Planes (1-3) ←── Fast vibration ──→ Instantaneous travel
        ↓
Middle Planes (4-5) ←── Moderate ──→ Thought-responsive
        ↓
   6th Plane ←── Transition ──→ The City/Mirage
        ↓
   7th Plane ←── Sluggish ──→ Viscous, time-dilated
```

### Specific Gravity Paradox
- **Terminology**: "Specific gravity" actually refers to **vibration level**
- **Measurement**: Planetary Regents monitor this, not mass
- **Transition requirement**: Must achieve sufficient vibration to "rise"
- **The Trap**: Heavy desires = low vibration = stuck

### Resonance Effects
| Vibration Interaction | Result |
|----------------------|--------|
| High meets Low | Repulsion or violent phase-shift |
| Like meets Like | Clumping (physical compression) |
| Living (high) + 7th entity | Gas-to-solid transition |
| 6th matter falls to 7th | Weight-stones (float upward) |

### Gas ↔ Solid Transitions

**The Addict's Reach (Gas → Solid)**:
1. Entity extends hand toward living person in bar
2. Appears as **wisp of dirty smoke** (gas phase)
3. Contacts living aura (higher vibration)
4. **Phase-shift**: Becomes **hardened claw** (solid phase)
5. Mechanism: Density increase on contact with higher energy

**Weight-Stones (Solid → Gas)**:
1. Chunks of 6th plane matter fall into 7th
2. **Lighter** than surrounding 7th matter
3. Float upward but **hot to touch**
4. Emit **screaming vibration** as crushed by density
5. Fate: Pulled back up or eventually crushed

Astral matter in 7th plane behaves like **viscous fluids**:
- **Tar**: Ground texture, semi-solidified
- **Mercury**: Movement resistance, waist-deep
- **Oil**: General viscosity, coating everything
- **Slime**: Entity surfaces, sticky

**Thoughts → Geometry**:
- Emotional spike creates vibration pattern
- Astral matter crystallizes around pattern
- Result: Sharp, jagged physical structures
- **Hate-Hooks**: Barbed, ballistic, tearing
- **Duration**: Flash (seconds) to Systemic (centuries)

### Vitrification
**Compression to Glass**:
- Under-earth city surfaces
- Astral matter compressed to diamond hardness
- **Result**: Impossibly smooth, glassy texture
- **Process**: Ordered vibration resisting chaotic grind

### Decay (The Second Death)
**Biological Process in Non-Biological Matter**:
1. Astral body "leaks" vital particles
2. Colors drain into surrounding miasma
3. Form becomes greyer, thinner
4. Skeletal frame of smoke appearance
