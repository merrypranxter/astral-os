# 7th Sub-Plane Entity Registry
## Complete Inhabitant Classification (VTAD)

## HUMAN DERIVATIVES

### The Grossly Material Deceased (Starving Ghosts)
**Classification**: Human dregs - entirely centered on grossest physical sensations

**Visual Fingerprint**:
- Bloated and darkened
- Viscous coating of murky browns and lurid pulsing reds
- Animalization: Face loses human definition, jaw heavier, eyes receding into predatory gleam
- No "light of ego" behind eyes

**Sensory Profile**:
- Feel: Heavy and burning
- Desire-matter vibrates at frantic, agonizing pace
- See physical world as dim grey fog with bright hot spots of indulgence

**Behavior**:
- Congregate around physical-world "vibration-nodes" of vice
- Slaughterhouses, dive bars, scenes of crime
- "Huddle" over living people, trying to "breathe in" the sensation
- The "Thirst" - constant unquenched craving

---

### Suicides and Victims of Sudden Death (The Unfinished)
**Classification**: Tragic class - locked in 7th plane with fully energized astral body

**The "Double" Sensation**:
- Intensely aware of physical body rotting
- Magnetic pull to scene of death
- "Divided" consciousness

**The Loop**:
- Forced to relive final moment over and over
- Thought-form of death becomes solid environment
- Cannot walk out of dense thought-construct

**Visuals**:
- Pale and frozen
- Surrounded by static-haze of final emotions

---

### The Shells (Astral Zombies)
**Classification**: Discarded husks - 3D recordings made of astral matter

**Physics**:
- No "Ego" or "Intelligence"
- Drift aimlessly repeating strongest habits
- Example: Man checking watch at 5PM seen making ghostly wrist motion

**The Galvanic Horror**:
- Can be reanimated by low-level Nature Spirits or Black Magicians
- Looks like person but eyes are hollow
- Feels: Cold and plastic to astral touch

---

### The Shades (Fading Memory-Traces)
**Classification**: Stage before shell fully discarded

**Visuals**:
- Faded sepia-toned photographs in 3D
- Moth-eaten blurry edges
- Watery insubstantiality

**Sensory**:
- **Sound**: Constant low-volume looping audio - scratched vinyl in next room
- **Touch**: Patch of cold damp mist - sudden temperature drop
- **Smell**: Dusty attics and old dry paper - the smell of forgetting

**Physics**:
- Highly magnetic to own past
- Hover over specific physical objects of obsession

---

### The Elementaries (True Inhabitants)
**Classification**: Souls of exceptionally depraved humans who severed higher connection

**Status**:
- Conscious, cunning, malicious
- The "True Inhabitants" of the 7th

**Tactics**:
- Vampirize energy of the living
- Whisper to vulnerable encouraging sudden impulses of rage or despair

**Visuals**:
- Jagged edges like broken glass
- Don't move - "flicker" from point to point

---

### Black Magicians (The Architects)
**Classification**: Most dangerous - learned to stay conscious to avoid Second Death

**Visuals**:
- Dense and metallic (unlike bloated addicts)
- Cloak in false light to appear as angels/guides to newly deceased

**Sensory**:
- Voice: Cold vibration like needle in center of consciousness
- Smell: Dry ozone and ancient dust

## NON-HUMAN ENTITIES

### Gnomes (Earth Elementals)
**Classification**: Engineers of the Mineral Kingdom

**Visuals**:
- Squat chunky entities
- Skin like unpolished anthracite or wet clay
- Eyes: Glittering facets of quartz or mica (not organic)
- No faces - only structural indentations

**Sensory**:
- **Feel**: Abrasive like coarse sandpaper, heavy to immovable point
- **Sound**: Grinding tectonic noise - rocks rubbing under immense pressure
- **Smell/Taste**: Petrichor and raw sulfur, bitter gritty like swallowing sand

**Physics**:
- Move through solid rock as thin liquid
- Work on ore veins guided by blind instinctual "Mineral Logic"

---

### Vampiric Nature Spirits
**Classification**: Animals of astral plane - no morality, only hunger

**Visuals**:
- Gelatinous spider-like or bat-winged shapes
- Translucent but stained dark bruised purple

**Habitat**:
- Vibrational shadows of physical locations where blood spilled
- Slaughterhouses, battlefields

**Sensory**:
- **Feel**: Sticky - leave residue acting as beacon for other predators
- **Smell**: Sharp cloying scent of raw iron and wet fur

**Function**: Scavengers feeding on Prana leaking from physical matter during trauma

## ARTIFICIAL ENTITIES (Thought-Forms)

### Hate-Hooks
**Origin**: Intense focused hatred knit into astral matter

**Visuals**:
- Jagged dart-like geometries or coiled serpentine shapes
- Lurid pulsating scarlet (color of cruelty)
- Barbs or hooks on leading edges

**Physics**:
- Ballistic velocity - seek target's astral body
- Rebound with doubled force if blocked by higher vibration

**Sensory**:
- **Touch**: Hot needle or serrated blade - tears at astral fabric
- **Sound**: High-pitched metallic hiss like steam escaping

---

### Vampiric Blobs (Addiction-Forms)
**Origin**: Intense physical cravings (alcoholism, drugs, predatory lust)

**Visuals**:
- Amoebic masses of dirty brown and swamp-green slime
- Translucent but cloudy like polluted water
- Filament-like tentacles drifting in currents

**Features**:
- Vortex-point "mouths" - depressions acting as vacuum cleaners

**Sensory**:
- **Smell**: Rotting vegetation and stale tobacco
- **Taste**: Bitter bile and copper

**Physics**:
- Sticky - gravitate to vibration-nodes of addiction
- Act as "Psychic Straw" sucking vital energy during indulgence

---

### Autonomous Nightmares (Terror-Forms)
**Origin**: Collective human panic or individual trauma

**Visuals**:
- Archetypal predator shapes
- Too many eyes
- Limbs moving in non-Euclidean geometry
- Shifting and protean

**Sensory**:
- **Texture**: Cold wet leather
- **Sound**: Low-frequency thrum inducing impending doom
- **Sensation**: Crawling feeling like hundreds of insects under astral skin

---

### Guardians of the Threshold (Personal Karma-Forms)
**Origin**: Composite of all individual's negative thoughts throughout life

**Visuals**:
- Monstrous distorted mirror-image of creator
- Clothed in muddy colors of 7th plane
- Caricature of one's own worst traits

**Properties**:
- **Weight**: Profoundly heavy - gravitational pull dragging creator down
- **Sound**: Creator's own voice distorted and layered like choir of regrets

---

### Elemental Slaves (Forced Constructs)
**Origin**: Gnomes captured and bound by Black Magicians

**Visuals**:
- Metallic statues of dull lead or iron
- Frozen until triggered

**Sensory**:
- **Feel**: Immovably solid - density itself
- **Smell**: Cold iron and dry clay

## SYSTEMIC ENTITIES

### Planetary Regents (Border Guards)
**Classification**: Lipika/Lords of Karma in lower-vibration forms

**Visuals**:
- Massive shimmering walls of geometric force
- Colossal unblinking eyes spanning horizon

**Function**:
- Maintain containment field
- Increase astral gravity
- Ensure no entity rises until specific gravity sufficient

**Sensory**:
- **Sound**: Constant sub-audible hum of Law being enforced
- **Feel**: Being crushed by invisible mountain - absolute clinical pressure

---

### Shadow-Entities (Gaps in the Light)
**Classification**: Not made of matter - anti-matter pockets

**Visuals**:
- Perfect 3D silhouettes of pure black
- Absorb murky light around them

**Physics**:
- Psychic sinkholes - drain color and vibration
- Absolute numbness on touch - temporary blackout of astral senses
- **Smell/Taste**: Nothing - complete absence of sensory data

---

### Deva-Dregs (Degraded Angelics)
**Classification**: Lower-order devas grounded due to magnetic error

**Visuals**:
- Tattered burning rags of gold light trapped in cage of black iron

**Aura**:
- Only true light in 7th plane but dim and flickering

**Sensory**:
- **Feel**: Hot and electric like live wire
- **Sound**: Faint mournful chiming like cracked crystal bell

---

### Vitalized Shells (Puppets)
**Classification**: Shells inhabited by minor elementals or nature spirits

**The Uncanny Valley**:
- Looks like person but movement wrong
- Staccato jerky motion
- Translucent plastic or wax skin

**The Tell - The Eyes**:
- Don't track naturally - snap to targets
- Hollow flickering glow inside skull (elemental occupant's light)

**Sensory**:
- **Touch**: Unnaturally smooth and cold
- **Smell**: Synthetic ozone and stagnant water

**Physics**:
- Used by lower mediums - memories worn like costume
- Plastic matter may stick to you momentarily (viscous parasitic grab)
